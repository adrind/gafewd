Hosting your project

• All the files (HTML/CSS/JS) that we've been writing in FEWD are "static" files.

• Hosting static files is quite simple:

⇒ Domain name (URL)

⇒ A place on the Internet to store our files

⇒ A way to "attach" the domain name to the storage location

Hosting your project

• More complex websites (with databases and servers) require more complex hosting solutions.

⇒ Google Domains

CLASS EXPECTATIONS

Here are some expectations we came up with together

AS A TEACHER

  • Provide consistent feedback. Homework that is turned in by the due date via Github will have feedback before the next class.
  • Admit when I don't know something. If I get stumped by a (good!) question, I promise to research and provide an answer in Slack.

AS A STUDENT

  • Reach out when I need help. If I am struggling or know I must miss a class, I will be proactive about reaching out to the instructor or my fellow students.
  • Have fun! It's cheesy, but the more you enjoy yourself the better you learn. Let's make this a fun learning environment for everyone.
